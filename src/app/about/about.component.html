About Us

How We Started

It all started with a vacation, a car, and a random name slapped on the back. Seeing that got me thinking—what if there was a better way to "review" someone's driving? Not just a phone number you’ll never call, but something quick, digital, and (most importantly) kind of ridiculous. Thus, Review My Driving was born. But I didn’t just want to make another boring feedback form... I wanted to get the last word in. A customizable "confirmation" message. A way to say "F you" or "Have a great day!" depending on how you feel.

Our Background

I’ve been a software engineer my whole life. From a young age, I knew I wanted to build things, and this project lets me combine that passion with a little creative chaos. There’s something truly exciting about taking a simple concept and turning it into an experience, one that’s equal parts frustrating and hilarious.

What We Do

At the end of the day, we all have thoughts about the way people drive, but there hasn’t really been a good way to share them—until now. Calling a number on the back of a truck? Not happening. But scanning a QR code and submitting a review? Much better. Review My Driving makes it easier (and slightly more entertaining) to give and get feedback. Whether you're here to rant, reflect, or just experience the worst form UI imaginable, welcome aboard.

Thank you for reading,
